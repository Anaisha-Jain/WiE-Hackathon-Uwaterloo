<h1>WiE Hackathon!</h1>
<ul>
    <li> Our project is called Tidal! </li>
    <li><b>Problem definition </b>:
  It's time we come out and say it: women’s bodies function biologically differently from men’s, and these differences have long been overlooked. We often fail to recognize that health is influenced by a variety of interconnected factors. Women in particular, face unique challenges shaped by cycles, hormones, and daily pressures. In fact, a global study of 3.3 million women found that the menstrual cycle has a stronger influence on mood, behavior, and vital signs than daily or seasonal patterns (ex: sleep/wake cycle).  </li>
    <li>Our goal is to bring holistic approach to healthcare and help women take care of themselves, even in the busy clutter of everyday life. At Tidal, we acknowledge the unique challenges women face and empower women to understand their patterns, embrace their bodies, and take their health in stride. 

We do this though implementing an AI chatbot into Tidal, where women can ask confidential questions and learn more about their bodies. Also, through the various graphics displayed on Tidal, making it easy to sift through medical jargon and understand their bodies on a deeper level. 
</li>
  </ul>
